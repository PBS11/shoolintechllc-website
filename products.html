Privacy First

Your Documents Stay on Your Device

Siftify processes documents locally using Llama 3.2 (1B parameters) running entirely on your phone. No document content is sent to any server unless you explicitly choose cloud analysis.

When cloud processing is needed for complex scanned documents, we show a clear consent modal before any data leaves your device — you're always in control.

Understands Every Document Type

Siftify uses specialized AI prompts for each document category — so a medical lab report is summarized differently from an immigration notice or a lease agreement.

  • ⚖️ Legal — Contracts, leases, deeds, court filings
  • 🏥 Medical — Health reports, lab results, patient records
  • 🛂 Immigration — USCIS notices, visas, work authorizations
  • 💰 Finance — Tax docs, statements, balance sheets
  • 📖 Manuals — User guides, technical documentation

Ask Questions, Hear Answers

Don't just read a summary — ask follow-up questions by voice. Siftify's voice Q&A feature lets you speak naturally and get targeted answers from your document's content.

Every section of the summary can be read aloud via text-to-speech, so you can listen while commuting, cooking, or on the go.
